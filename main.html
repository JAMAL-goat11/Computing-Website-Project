Student Rights and Obligations - Elyon Christian School

It explains that every student should have the right to learn in a safe and fair enviroment,to get support from teachers, to use school facilities and to grow even more by attending CCA (Co-Curricular Activities)

Students have the right to learn in a enviroment that shows christian values. This means that schools shouldn't only be about learning but also growing as a person. They are encouraged to practice kindness and integrity in their daily lives.

STUDENT RIGHT
  1. Participate in academic and non-academic activities conducted both inside and outside the school, including online and onsite formats.
  2. Receive religious education in accordance with Christian values.
  3. Utilize allschool facilities appropriately,according to their intended functionsand regulations.
  4. Receive equal treatment from the school principal,teachers, and staff.
  5. Access guidance and counseling services from counselors or teachers basedon needs and procedures.
  6. Receive health services provided by the school clinic in accordance with need sand regulations.
  7. Respond to and defend against any allegations made toward them.
  8. Develop themselves by participating in Co-Curricular Activities(CCA) provided by the school.
  9. Take remedial or make-up assessments as approved by subject teachers based on needs and guidelines.
  10. Receive feedback or results on assignments or assessments within two weeksat the latest.
  11. Submit constructive suggestions or feedback to the school leadership,teachers, or staff in accordance with proper procedures and ethics.
  12. Participate in competitions or events on behalf of the school with the principal’s approval.
  13. Receive information and take partin selection processesfor self-development opportunities (scholarships, competitions, student exchange programs).
  14. Receive report slips or diplomas regularly, provided all administrative payments and returns of borrowed school property have been fulfilled.
  15. Receive report card or diplomas upon withdrawal or graduation, provided that all administrative obligations and the return of borrowed property havebeen completed.
STUDENT OBLIGATIONS
  1. Receive report card or diplomas upon withdrawal or graduation, provided that all administrative obligations and the return of borrowed property have been completed.
  2. Comply with allschool rules, both written and verbal.
  3. Show respect to all members of the school community through polite behavior and speech.
  4. Be present atschool on time during in-person learning.
  5. Participate in all classes and school programs.
  6. Informand request permission when absent;five consecutive unexcused absences are considered a withdrawal.
  7. Ask for clarification or updates on lessons or school activities that were missed or not understood.
  8. Come prepared with books and learning tools asrequired.
  9. Actively participate in school events and activities.
  10. Maintain cleanliness, a comfortable environment, and classroom order.
  11. Be responsible for school property and replace any damage caused by negligence or intent.
  12. Take care of personal belongings atschool (mobile phones must be submitted to the homeroom teacher).
